Learning Outcome 6: Biology Connections

Biology connections are our personal links to the study of life, and our everyday experiences as we attempt to be lifelong learners. I have two projects that demonstrate personal connections I have made. The first is a lifelong bucket list I want to take up and try to finish. It involves a couple collections, many trips I wouldn't otherwise take, and participation in local communities and citizen science endeavors I wouldn't be as interested in without a larger goal. The second piece is the seven steps technique, which is a pretty quick and easy everyday science tool I can take wherever I go into nature to better synthesis and get a basic understanding of an area I am in.

Media 1: Life long biology bucket list

My biology bucket list. I think it has a nice spread of goals that honestly encourage me to partake in a nice range of local and global experiences.

Media 2: Seven Steps technique

The 7-steps technique is a really nice tool to have in every day life, as it only takes a couple minutes and is agnostic to the environment you are in. I feel it really gets your eyes focused on the right kinds of things to look for ecology and cool quirks of nature in an area.
