Learning Outcome 5: Biology Skills

Biology skills are the tools and methods which we use to understand and study living organisms and the means with which they function. To show my proficiency in this I have collected a quadrat sampling I took, alongside with data of what I found in the sample, and a pie chart to help visualize it. Along with this I have a response on why sampling is a good tool, what it's limitations are, and where it should and shouldn't be applied.
