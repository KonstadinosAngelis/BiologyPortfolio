Learning Outcome 1: Science Concepts

The concepts of science are our foundational ideas and practices that help us understand and learn more about the world. Some of the parts I enjoyed learning about the most include my pressed leaf specimen, predicting how latitudes and rain shadow affect climate and vegetation growth, and a hypothetical where I predict the cause & effect of an exploratory study.
